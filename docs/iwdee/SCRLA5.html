      Melf's Minute Meteors
(Evocation, Alteration)

Level: 3
Range: 0
Duration: Permanent until thrown or dispelled
Casting Time: 3
Area of Effect: The caster
Saving Throw: None

This spell creates small globes of fire, one for each experience level the wizard has attained, which are held in the wizard's hands in place of  usual weapons. The meteors can be hurled at targets up to visual range and are treated as missiles with a +5 bonus to the attack rolls. Each meteor bursts into a fiery sphere upon impact, inflicting 1d4+3 points of piercing damage and 3 points of fire damage to any creature struck. The wizard can hurl up to five missiles per round.
