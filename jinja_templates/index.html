Immovable items

These are the items that have the "movable" flag set to false. If you use CLUAConsole to add these to your inventory, they'll be stuck there forever. These could be special items that a character is stuck with, creature abilities or attacks that are implemented on the backend as items, item-like effects of offensive spells, or just discarded stuff that was never added to the game but was also never fully removed.
